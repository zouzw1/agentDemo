# Industry Analyst

A comprehensive industry analysis skill that examines company sector development, competitive positioning, and strategic insights based on specific analytical frameworks.

## Quick start

"Analyze the electric vehicle industry in China, focusing on market trends and competitive landscape."

## Instructions

Follow these steps to conduct a comprehensive industry analysis:

### Part 1: Industry Development Overview

1. **Analyze Market Trends & Development Status**:
    - Research current market size and growth trajectory
    - Review historical performance (last 3 years minimum)
    - Identify future projections and emerging opportunities
    - Document key growth drivers and constraints

2. **Conduct Geographic Analysis**:
    - Focus on specified region (default: China)
    - Assess development situation in target regions
    - Evaluate competitive positioning within markets
    - Analyze changes in competitive standing over past three years

3. **Identify Industry Characteristics**:
    - Document key operational features specific to the industry
    - Determine market maturity level (emerging, growing, mature, declining)
    - Note seasonal or cyclical patterns affecting operations
    - Review relevant regulatory environment and compliance requirements

4. **Assess Industrial Situation**:
    - Analyze supply and demand dynamics
    - Evaluate production capacity utilization rates
    - Review import/export balance and trade patterns
    - Examine upstream and downstream relationships in value chain

### Part 2: Industry Feature Analysis

5. **Evaluate Policy Environment**:
    - Identify relevant government policies and initiatives
    - Analyze specific measures affecting the industry
    - Assess regulatory changes and compliance requirements
    - Evaluate policy impact on industry development

6. **Map Industry Competition Landscape**:
    - Identify key players and their market share distribution
    - Assess barriers to entry and exit
    - Analyze consolidation trends and M&A activity
    - Evaluate competitive strategies employed by major players

7. **Determine Core Competitiveness Factors**:
    - Identify key success factors in the industry
    - Evaluate critical resources and capabilities needed
    - Analyze differentiation opportunities
    - Assess sustainable competitive advantages

8. **Analyze Global Industry Position**:
    - Research worldwide development status
    - Evaluate international competitive advantages/disadvantages
    - Assess export potential and global market positioning
    - Identify technology transfer opportunities

9. **Conduct Technical Analysis**:
    - Map technology roadmap and iteration status
    - Analyze innovation trends and R&D investment patterns
    - Evaluate intellectual property landscape
    - Assess digital transformation progress and adoption

10. **Identify Industry Challenges**:
    - Document current problems and pain points
    - Conduct risk factors and vulnerability assessment
    - Evaluate sustainability concerns and ESG factors
    - Assess supply chain resilience and potential disruptions
