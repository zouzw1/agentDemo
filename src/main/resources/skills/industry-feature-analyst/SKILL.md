# Industry Feature Analyst

Provides deep-dive analysis of specific industry characteristics including policy environment, competition landscape, core competitiveness factors, and technical assessment.

## Quick start

"Analyze the competitive landscape and policy environment for the artificial intelligence industry."

## Instructions

1. **Analyze policy environment**:
   - Identify relevant government policies
   - Assess specific measures affecting the industry
   - Evaluate regulatory changes and compliance requirements
   - Analyze policy impact on industry development

2. **Map competition landscape**:
   - Identify key players and market share distribution
   - Assess barriers to entry
   - Analyze consolidation trends
   - Evaluate competitive strategies

3. **Assess core competitiveness factors**:
   - Identify key success factors in the industry
   - Evaluate critical resources and capabilities needed
   - Analyze differentiation opportunities
   - Assess sustainable competitive advantages

4. **Evaluate global industry position**:
   - Analyze worldwide development status
   - Assess international competitive advantages/disadvantages
   - Evaluate export potential and global market positioning
   - Identify technology transfer opportunities

5. **Conduct technical analysis**:
   - Map technology roadmap and iteration status
   - Analyze innovation trends and R&D investment
   - Evaluate intellectual property landscape
   - Assess digital transformation progress

6. **Identify industry challenges**:
   - Analyze current problems and pain points
   - Conduct risk factors and vulnerability assessment
   - Evaluate sustainability concerns
   - Assess supply chain resilience

7. **Generate feature analysis report**:
   - Provide detailed analysis by feature category
   - Include strategic implications
   - Offer actionable recommendations
   - Identify risk mitigation strategies
