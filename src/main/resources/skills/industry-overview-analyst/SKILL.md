## Quick start

"Analyze the electric vehicle industry development in China over the past 3 years."

## Instructions

1. **Gather industry data**:
   - Collect market size and growth data
   - Review historical performance (last 3 years)
   - Compile future projections

2. **Conduct geographic analysis**:
   - Focus on China and specific regions as requested
   - Analyze competitive positioning
   - Evaluate changes in competitive standing over past three years

3. **Analyze industry characteristics**:
   - Identify key operational features
   - Assess market maturity level
   - Note seasonal or cyclical patterns
   - Review regulatory environment

4. **Evaluate industrial situation**:
   - Analyze supply and demand dynamics
   - Assess production capacity utilization
   - Review import/export balance
   - Examine upstream and downstream relationships

5. **Generate comprehensive report**:
   - Provide executive summary
   - Present detailed analysis by category
   - Include data visualization recommendations
   - Offer strategic insights
