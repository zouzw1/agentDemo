
# Comprehensive Skills Collection

## 1. Industry Analysis Skill

### Skill Name: industry-analyst

### Description:
A comprehensive industry analysis skill that examines company sector development, competitive positioning, and strategic insights based on specific analytical frameworks.

### Parameters:
- company_name (string): Name of the company to analyze
- industry_sector (string): Primary industry sector of the company
- geographic_focus (string): Geographic region to focus on (default: China)

### Functionality:

#### Part 1: Industry Development Overview
Analyzes the overall development situation of细分 industries that the company belongs to, including:

1. **Market Trends & Development Status**
   - Current market size and growth trajectory
   - Historical performance (last 3 years)
   - Future projections and emerging opportunities

2. **Geographic Analysis**
   - Development situation in China and specific regions
   - Competitive position within China and regional markets
   - Changes in competitive standing over the past three years

3. **Industry Characteristics**
   - Key operational features
   - Market maturity level
   - Seasonal or cyclical patterns
   - Regulatory environment

4. **Industrial Situation**
   - Supply and demand dynamics
   - Production capacity utilization
   - Import/export balance
   - Upstream and downstream relationships

#### Part 2: Industry Feature Analysis
Provides deeper insights into current industry characteristics:

1. **Policy Environment**
   - Relevant government policies
   - Specific measures affecting the industry
   - Regulatory changes and compliance requirements

2. **Industry Competition Landscape**
   - Key players and market share distribution
   - Barriers to entry
   - Consolidation trends
   - Competitive strategies

3. **Core Competitiveness Factors**
   - Key success factors in the industry
   - Critical resources and capabilities needed
   - Differentiation opportunities

4. **Global Industry Position**
   - Worldwide development status
   - International competitive advantages/disadvantages
   - Export potential and global market positioning
   - Technology transfer opportunities

5. **Technical Analysis**
   - Technology roadmap and iteration status
   - Innovation trends and R&D investment
   - Intellectual property landscape
   - Digital transformation progress

6. **Industry Challenges**
   - Current problems and pain points
   - Risk factors and vulnerability assessment
   - Sustainability concerns
   - Supply chain resilience

### Output Format:
The skill will generate a comprehensive report with:
- Executive summary
- Detailed analysis by category
- Data visualization recommendations
- Strategic recommendations
- Future outlook

### Dependencies:
- Access to industry databases
- Government statistics
- Market research reports
- Company financial data

## 2. Market Intelligence Skill

### Skill Name: market-intelligence

### Description:
Gathers and analyzes market intelligence data to support strategic business decisions.

### Parameters:
- market_type (string): B2B, B2C, or hybrid market
- competitors (list): List of competitor companies to analyze
- timeframe (string): Time period to analyze (e.g., 1Y, 3Y, 5Y)

### Functionality:
- Competitor analysis
- Market trend identification
- Consumer behavior insights
- Pricing strategy analysis
- Channel effectiveness assessment

## 3. Financial Performance Skill

### Skill Name: financial-performance

### Description:
Analyzes company financial performance and compares it with industry benchmarks.

### Parameters:
- company_symbol (string): Stock symbol of the company
- fiscal_years (int): Number of years to analyze
- comparison_set (list): Companies for benchmarking

### Functionality:
- Revenue analysis and forecasting
- Profitability ratios evaluation
- Cash flow assessment
- Debt and leverage analysis
- ROE/ROA comparison with peers

## 7. Sustainability Analysis Skill
